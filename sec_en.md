# Detailed Classification of SEC Filings (For Public Companies)

This classification provides a comprehensive overview of the various filings that public companies must submit under the requirements of the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). The filings are organized into the following main categories: Registration and Issuance Filings, Periodic Reports, Insider and Institutional Disclosures, Proxy and Corporate Governance Filings, Tender Offer & M&A Filings, Investment Company Filings, and Miscellaneous Filings. Filings with a “/A” suffix indicate an amended or supplement version.

 

## 1. Registration and Issuance Filings  
(Used for registering securities and raising capital under the Securities Act of 1933 and related rules)

### 1.1 Under the Securities Act of 1933 (S-Series)
- **Form S-1**: Initial registration statement for an IPO  
  - Amendment: S-1/A  
- **Form S-2**: Registration statement used by companies with some operating history  
- **Form S-3**: Simplified registration statement for seasoned companies  
  - Amendment: S-3/A  
- **Form S-4**: Registration statement for mergers, exchange offers, and other business combination transactions  
  - Amendment: S-4/A  
- **Form S-6**: Registration statement for unit investment trusts  
- **Form S-8**: Registration statement for securities offered under employee benefit plans  
- **Form S-11**: Registration statement for Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs) or real estate companies  
  - Amendment: S-11/A

### 1.2 Filings for Foreign Private Issuers (F-Series)
- **Form F-1**: Initial registration statement for foreign private issuers  
  - Amendment: F-1/A  
- **Form F-3**: Simplified registration statement for mature foreign issuers  
  - Amendment: F-3/A  
- **Form F-4**: Registration statement used by foreign private issuers in merger transactions  
  - Amendment: F-4/A  
- **Form F-6**: Registration statement for American Depositary Receipts (ADRs)  
  - Amendment: F-6/A  
  - Auto-effective version: F-6EF  
- **Form F-7**: Registration statement for certain Canadian issuers offering securities for cash  
  - Amendment: F-7/A  
- **Form F-8**: Registration statement for Canadian issuers used in exchange or merger transactions  
  - Amendment: F-8/A  
- **Form F-9**: Registration statement for certain investment-grade debt or preferred securities of Canadian issuers  
  - Amendment: F-9/A  
- **Form F-10** and **Form F-80**: Other registration documents for Canadian issuers  
  - (Amended versions as applicable, e.g. F-10/A)

### 1.3 Regulation A Filings  
- **Form 1-A**: Offering statement under Regulation A  
  - Amendment: 1-A/A  
- **Form 1-A POS**: Post-effective amendment to a Form 1-A filing  
- **Form 1-A-W**: Withdrawal statement for a Form 1-A filing  
  - Amendment: 1-A-W/A

 

## 2. Periodic Report Filings  
(These filings provide up-to-date information on the company's financial and operational status)

### 2.1 Annual Reports  
- **Form 10-K**: Annual report for U.S. companies; includes audited financial statements, business overview, risk factors, MD&A, etc.  
  - Amendment: 10-K/A  
  - Variants (e.g., 10-K405 and 10-K405/A) may also be used.  
- **Form 20-F**: Annual (and transition) report for foreign private issuers  
  - Amendment: 20-F/A  
- **Form 40-F**: Annual report for certain Canadian companies  
  - Amendment: 40-F/A  
- Investment company reports such as the **NSAR series** (e.g., NSAR-A, NSAR-B, NSAR-B/A, NSAR-BT, NSAR-U)

### 2.2 Quarterly Reports  
- **Form 10-Q**: Quarterly report for U.S. companies (unaudited)  
  - Amendment: 10-Q/A  
- **Form 10-KT / 10-KT/A**: Transition reports related to the annual report  
- **Form 10-QT / 10-QT/A**: Transition versions of the quarterly report  
- **Form 10-QSB**: Simplified quarterly report for small business issuers  
  - Amendment: 10-QSB/A

### 2.3 Current (Event) Reports  
- **Form 8-K**: Current report disclosing material events or changes  
  - Amendment: 8-K/A  
- **Form 6-K**: Current report used by foreign issuers to disclose significant events  
  - Amendment: 6-K/A

 

## 3. Insider and Institutional Disclosures  
(For reporting insider holdings, transactions, and large institutional positions)

### 3.1 Insider Disclosures
- **Form 3**: Initial statement of beneficial ownership for insiders  
- **Form 4**: Report of insider trading and changes in holdings  
  - (Sometimes also available as 4/A if amended)  
- **Form 5**: Annual insider trading report  
  - Amendment: 5/A

### 3.2 Institutional and Large Trader Filings  
- **Form 13F Series**:  
  - **13F-HR**: Quarterly holdings report filed by institutional investment managers  
    - Amendment: 13F-HR/A  
  - **13F-NT**: Institutional holdings notice report  
    - Amendment: 13F-NT/A  
  - **13FCONP**: Another version of the institutional holdings report  
- **Form 13H Series**:  
  - **13H**, **13H-Q**, **13H-A**, **13H-I**, **13H-R**, **13H-T**: Required for large traders and significant investors

 

## 4. Proxy and Corporate Governance Filings  
(Used to provide information for shareholder voting and corporate governance matters)

### 4.1 Definitive Proxy Statements and Information Filings  
- **Form DEF 14A**: Definitive proxy statement  
- **Form DEF 14C**: Definitive information statement (when proxy voting is not involved)  
- **Other related filings**:  
  - **DEFA14A**: Additional proxy soliciting material from non-management  
  - **DEFA14C**: Additional information statement materials from non-management  
  - **DEFC14A** and **DEFC14C**: Proxy or information statements for contested situations  
  - **DEFM14A** and **DEFM14C**: Definitive proxy/information filings in merger or acquisition contexts  
  - **DEFN14A**: Proxy statement filed by non-management insiders  
  - **DEFR14A** and **DEFR14C**: Revised or amended proxy/information statements

### 4.2 Preliminary and Revised Proxy Materials  
- Preliminary/ Revised series including:  
  - **PRE 14A**, **PRE 14C**, **PREC14A**, **PREC14C**, **PREM14A**, **PREM14C**, **PREN14A**, **PRER14A**, **PRER14C**, **PRRN14A**

 

## 5. Tender Offers and M&A Filings  
(Filings required in connection with mergers, acquisitions, and tender offers)

### 5.1 Acquisition Disclosures  
- **Schedule 13D**: Disclosure statement for acquiring more than 5% of a company’s voting shares  
  - Amendment: 13D/A
- **Schedule 13G**: Simplified disclosure for passive investors  
  - Amendment: 13G/A

### 5.2 Tender Offer and Merger Filings  
- **Tender Offer Filings**:  
  - Examples include filings under **SC 14D9** (and SC 14D9/A), **SC 14F1** (and SC 14F1/A)  
  - Third-party tender offer filings such as **Schedule TO-T**  
- **Additional Merger Filings**:  
  - **Form S-4**: Registration statement used in merger/exchange offers  
    - Amendment: S-4/A, and variants such as S-4MEF

 

## 6. Investment Company Filings  
(Filings specific to mutual funds, closed-end funds, and other investment companies)

### 6.1 Registration Filings for Investment Companies  
- **Form N-1A**: Registration statement for open-end management investment companies (mutual funds)  
  - Amendment: N-1A/A  
- **Form N-2**: Registration statement for closed-end investment companies  
  - Amendment: N-2/A  
- **Form N-3**: Registration statement for separate accounts (management investment companies)  
  - Amendment: N-3/A  
- **Form N-4**: Registration statement for unit investment trusts  
  - Amendment: N-4/A  
- **Form N-5**: Registration statement for small business investment companies  
- **Form N-14**: Registration statement for investment companies in mergers or business combination transactions  
  - Variants: N-14, N-14/A, N-14 8C, N-14 8C/A, N-14AE (and its amendment)

- **Forms 485APOS / 485BPOS**: Post-effective amendments to the investment company registration statement

### 6.2 Investment Company Periodic Reports and Proxy Filings  
- **Form N-CSR**: Certified annual shareholder report  
  - Amendment: N-CSR/A  
- **Form N-CSRS**: Certified semiannual shareholder report  
  - Amendment: N-CSRS/A  
- **Form N-PX**: Annual proxy voting record report for management investment companies  
  - Amendment: N-PX/A  
- **Form N-Q**: Quarterly report of portfolio holdings  
  - Amendment: N-Q/A  
- Additionally, the **NSAR series** (e.g., NSAR-A, NSAR-B, NSAR-BT, NSAR-U) are used

 

## 7. Miscellaneous Filings  
(Other filings including amendments, withdrawal/termination notices, and specific reporting forms)

### 7.1 Amendments, Notices, and Withdrawals  
- **General Amendment Files**: Most filings have an amended version (denoted by “/A”)  
- **Termination or Suspension Notices**:  
  - Examples: 15-12B, 15-12B/A, 15-12G, 15-12G/A, 15-15D, 15-15D/A  
  - For foreign private issuers: 15F-12B, 15F-12B/A, 15F-12G, 15F-12G/A, 15F-15D, 15F-15D/A

### 7.2 Rule 144 Filings  
- **Form 144**: Notice for proposed sale of restricted securities under Rule 144  
  - Amendment: 144/A

### 7.3 Post-Effective and Special Purpose Filings  
- **POS Series Filings**:  
  - Examples: POS 8C, POS AM, POS AMI, POSASR, POS462B, POS462C  
  - These are used to amend an already effective registration statement  
- **CORRESP**: Correspondence filings with the SEC  
- **Other Specific Filings**:  
  - E.g., Form 10-D for asset-backed issuers; Forms 1-E, 1-E AD, 2-E for sales materials, etc.  
- **Withdrawal/Revocation Filings**:  
  - Examples: AW, AW WD, RW, RW WD

> **Note:**  
> 1. This detailed classification focuses solely on the filings required for public companies. Filings used in private placements (such as Form D) are not included.  
> 2. The “/A” suffix always indicates an amendment or supplement to the original filing.  
> 3. The SEC EDGAR system contains hundreds of filing types; the list above covers the principal and commonly used forms for public companies.
